Eevee Evolution

How to evolve your Eevee


You will be given a random number at the start of your turn.

There are four evolution stones below. By clicking on a stone you will add a specific amount of points to your Eevee Evoluton Points.

You win the game by matching your Eevee Evolution Points to the Points Needed to Evolve Your Eevee.

Your Eevee does not evolve if your Eevee Evolution Points goes pass the Points Needed to Evolve Your Eevee.

The value of each stone is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, or restarts, the game will change the value of each stone.
