Eevee Evolution

How to evolve your Eevee


You will be given a random number at the start of your turn.

There are four evolution stones below. By clicking on a stone you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if you total score goes above the random number.

The value of each stone is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each stone.
